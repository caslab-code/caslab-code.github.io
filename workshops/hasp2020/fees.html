Publication Fees

HASP 2020 will have a suggested publication fee of 150 USD for papers accepted and published in the proceedings. The fee will be used to cover ACM Digital Library publication fees. We have unrecovered ACM expenses of 843.90 USD from prior workshop and expected expenses of about 1000.00 USD for HASP 2020. The suggested fee will be 150 USD for each paper. Most researchers have NSF or other funding which they can use for publication fees; we will not reject papers who are not able to pay, but strongly encourage everybody to pay as otherwise we are not able to continue to publish the articles associated with HASP.
