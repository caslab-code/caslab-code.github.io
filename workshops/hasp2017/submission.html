Hardware and Architectural Support for Security and Privacy (HASP) 2017

To be held Sunday, June 25, 2017 in Toronto, Canada -- in conjunction with ISCA 2017

Submission Information

Submissions must be written in English with at most 8 pages including the bibliography and appendices. Submissions must be in double-column ISCA 2017 format. All accepted and presented papers will be included in the ACM Digital Library. The proceedings will be published in ACM ICPS, the ISBN number assigned to HASP is 978-1-4503-5266-6.

Electronic Submission Information

Papers can be submitted electronically via the EasyChair web page: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=hasp2017.
