CASLAB Day 2018 will be on April 19th (Thursday) from 9am to Noon at the Mann Engineering Student Center (Room 107), 10 Hillhouse Ave., New Haven, CT.

The event is open to the public.

The event will be followed directly by EE department luncheon also in Mann Engineering Student Center.

-
10:00		 Breakfast and Poster Session

Posters will be presented by CASLAB graduate students describing current research. This year's topics include:

* Hardware Architecture Security Verification
* Applications of Physically Unclonable Functions based on DRAM
* Post-Quantum Cryptographic Algorithm Implementation on FPGAs
10:00
-
11:00		 Tutorial: Principles of Secure Processor Architecture Design -- Part 1

The tutorial will overview the so-called “secure processor architectures”, present design patterns that can be gleaned from the existing research works, and will derive the basic principles that inform design of the secure processor architectures. Trusted execution environments, memory integrity and other topics will be covered.
